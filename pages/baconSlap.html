The recently trending “Bacon Slap Challenge” is catching a lot of steam as it aims to increase awareness for bacon farmers and security teams around the world. Farmers and farm security die every day, being butchered by pigs, trying to extract bacon and distribute a cure for cancer; truly selfless work. The BSC consists of slapping someone in the name of raising funds to research better security measures. When you get slapped you can choose to donate and slap your other friends to raise awareness or you keep getting slapped until you donate, sometimes getting slapped even if you donate, raising the bar for how much people are really enjoying this challenge and the benefits it brings to the world.

The BSC has currently raised $40,000 for research and it’s getting bigger every day. Do yourself and your people a favour and go out there and slap someone in the name of bacon!

In following these simple guidelines together we can build a hospitable and better planet.
