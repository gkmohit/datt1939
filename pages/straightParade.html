Ella DeStingy takes Lead in Straight Pride Parade An estimate of 100 people walked and danced celebrating Straight Pride Parade yesterday. The masses were lead but popular figure Ella DeStingy. Ella DeStingy recently went into the closet which took the world and her fans by storm. “I was born straight and it was about time that the world started to accept us straight people, we are human too.” stated Ella during her last interview. Apart from leading the parade Ella also funded a lot towards it, such as investing millions to design the Straight Pride flag. She said “There was a need for a symbol, to unite us all, a sigh of hope to lift our spirits and bring joy to all.” (image below)

The much looked forward to event can to an end with a display of gray scaled fireworks. Many were involved and all too back memories to remember for a life time.
